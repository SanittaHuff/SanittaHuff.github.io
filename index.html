Welcome to The Makers Market! The Makers Market is a place for dreamers and makers to meet and collaborate to make wonderful things possible. The "Makers" area is for posting images, links and tutorials on custom made items such as jewlery and ferniture. The "Dreamers" area is for posting ideas and inovations which challenge makers to showcase their skills.
